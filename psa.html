Public Service Announcements

Major Update

Quick paragraph

WebGFA will get a major update. To get games, you will have to pay for them with GFA-Coins. To get GFA-Coins you can watch ads or pay for them with cash. One random game will be free to play each day. If you paid before then you will recieve the amount you paid for in GFA-Coins. You start with 100 GFA-Coins and will be able to buy games in a auction. The games can be Roblox for 1 hour, or Vex 8 for 3 days.

How to get GFA-Coins

To get GFA-Coins you can buy them with money, 100 GFA-Coins for 1$, or watching advertisments.

How to play games

When you first create an account, you will receive 1 hour of gameplay time for all games. Gameplay time is considered in when you first open up the game page, and when you close it. For all games means that it is combined time, so 30 mins of Cookie Clicker and 30 mins of Retro Bowl will amount to 1 hour of all games. To get more games a auction will be reccuring where different games are avalibe to play as a bidding. Also, you will get one random game to play each day for free.

Other events

If at a certain time when specified, like for example 2:00pm and a certain number of users are playing the site then all the users online will recieve a gift. To be notifeid of these events the webpage will ask you for notifications. Click yes, and you will get notified when the events are happening.
